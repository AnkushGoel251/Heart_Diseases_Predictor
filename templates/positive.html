Congrats! ,You have low chances of heart diseases

Follow these advice and keep having regular checkups for better health

Diet Recommendation :-

  • Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables: Aim for at least five servings of fruits and vegetables per day. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support heart health.
  • Choose whole grains: Opt for whole grain products like whole wheat bread, brown rice, and oats. They are high in fiber and can help lower cholesterol levels.
  • Include healthy fats: Consume sources of healthy fats such as avocados, nuts, seeds, and fatty fish like salmon. These fats contain omega-3 fatty acids that can help reduce the risk of heart disease.

Exercise advice :-

  • Aerobic exercises: Engage in aerobic activities such as brisk walking, jogging, cycling, swimming, or dancing. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per week.
  • Strength training: Include strength training exercises at least twice a week. It helps build muscle, improve metabolism, and support overall cardiovascular health.
  • Find activities you enjoy: Choose physical activities that you genuinely enjoy. It increases the likelihood of sticking to a regular exercise routine.
